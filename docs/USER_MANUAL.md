# User Manual 

Welcome to the **Choice Intelligence Platform**! This dashboard is your central hub for monitoring, analyzing, and deriving actionable insights from video content published across all Choice Group brands.

This manual will walk you through the core features of the platform and how to get the most out of them.

---

## 📊 1. The Statistics Dashboard

The **Statistics** page is the homepage of the application. It provides a high-level executive summary of your entire social media footprint.

### Key Features:
- **Executive Summary Cards**: Displays real-time, absolute lifetime totals of your Videos, Comments, Likes, and Views across all brands.
- **Top Performing Content**: A horizontally scrolling list of the top 10 best-performing videos across the entire company. You can click the "Watch Video" button on any card to jump directly to the original video (e.g., YouTube).
- **Brand Comparison**: A dynamic chart allowing you to compare brand performance side-by-side. Use the "Filter by brand" dropdown to isolate a specific entity.

---

## 🔍 2. Content Explorer

The **Content Explorer** acts as your central database for every piece of content that the platform has scraped.

### Key Features:
- **Search & Filter**: You can search for specific video titles, filter by a specific Choice Group brand, or filter by a specific publication date range.
- **Data Table**: A clean list showing the Video Thumbnail, Title, Brand, Publish Date, and raw Engagement Metrics. *Note: The date displayed is the actual date the video was published on the platform, not the date our system scraped it.*
- **Detailed View**: Clicking anywhere on a table row opens the Detailed View. This expands to show full stats, an engagement score calculation, and an expandable "Engagement History" tab (hidden by default) which tracks how a video's metrics changed over time.
- **Scraper Status**: At the bottom of the page, you can see real-time details about the data scraper. It includes the last successful run time, next scheduled run, and a collapsible menu to view the exact videos fetched during the most recent session.

---

## 🧠 3. AI Analytics

The **AI Analytics** page uses Advanced Large Language Models to read through thousands of comments and metrics to give you actionable human-readable intelligence.

### How to use it:
1. Navigate to the AI Analytics tab.
2. Select a specific brand from the dropdown (or leave it blank for all brands).
3. Select a date range. 
   - *Tip: If you leave the dates blank, the AI will automatically analyze all data from the beginning of time up to today.*
4. Click **Generate Analysis**. 
5. Because the AI has to read a massive amount of data, a progress bar will appear. You are free to navigate away to other pages while this runs in the background!
6. Once complete, you will see cards detailing:
   - **Audience Intelligence**: What viewers love, dislike, and are asking for.
   - **Content Intelligence**: Trending topics, brand focus areas, active campaigns, and product launches.

You can click **Clear Analysis** at any time to remove the current report from your screen and generate a new one.

---

## ✨ 4. Floating AI Assistant

Available on **every page** in the bottom-right corner is your personal **Floating AI Assistant**.

### How to use it:
- Click the floating circular sparkle (✨) icon.
- You can ask the AI highly specific questions about your data. For example:
  - *"Which brand had the highest engagement this month?"*
  - *"What are people saying about the new Choice Wealth launch?"*
- **Persistent Memory**: The chat panel preserves your conversation history. You can minimize the chat, navigate to a different page, and reopen it without losing your conversation!
